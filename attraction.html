Popular Vancouver Destinations

Click any photo to view a larger image.

Canada Place

A famous Vancouver waterfront landmark with beautiful harbour views and the iconic white sails.

Stanley Park

A world-famous urban park featuring forests, seawalls, beaches, gardens, and scenic views.

Gastown

Vancouver's historic district known for cobblestone streets, restaurants, and the Steam Clock.

Granville Island

A waterfront destination with markets, shops, restaurants, and local art.

Capilano Suspension Bridge

A spectacular rainforest attraction featuring a famous suspension bridge above the canyon.

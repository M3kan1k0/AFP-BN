Vancouver & British Columbia Attractions

Make your Batang Nichols Reunion 2027 even more memorable by exploring some of the most beautiful destinations in British Columbia. Most attractions are within a short drive from the reunion venue, while others are perfect for a day trip or extended vacation.

Welcome to Beautiful British Columbia

Whether you're visiting from the Philippines or traveling from another province, Vancouver offers stunning parks, mountains, gardens, waterfronts, and unforgettable day trips. Click any photo to view it full screen.

🌆 Vancouver Highlights

🌊 Waterfront Landmark

Canada Place

Vancouver's iconic waterfront landmark featuring the famous white sails, cruise ship terminal, and beautiful views of Burrard Inlet.

🚗 Approx. 20 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps
🌲 Nature

Stanley Park

One of Vancouver's most famous attractions. Enjoy forests, beaches, seawall walks, totem poles, and spectacular city views.

🚗 Approx. 25 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps
🏛 Historic District

Gastown

Vancouver's oldest neighbourhood, known for cobblestone streets, historic buildings, restaurants, and the famous Steam Clock.

🚗 Approx. 20 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps
🎨 Market & Culture

Granville Island

A popular waterfront destination with public markets, restaurants, shops, galleries, and scenic views.

🚗 Approx. 20 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps
🌉 Adventure

Capilano Suspension Bridge

A world-famous rainforest attraction featuring a spectacular suspension bridge, forest trails, and breathtaking canyon views.

🚗 Approx. 30 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps

🏔 Mountains & Nature

🏔 Mountain Adventure

Grouse Mountain

A popular North Vancouver destination with spectacular views of Vancouver, hiking trails, wildlife, and the famous Skyride gondola.

🚗 Approx. 35 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps
🏔 Scenic Views

Cypress Mountain

A beautiful mountain destination offering hiking, viewpoints, winter activities, and panoramic views of Vancouver and Howe Sound.

🚗 Approx. 45 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps
🌊 Waterfront Park

Rocky Point Park

A scenic waterfront park in Port Moody featuring Burrard Inlet views, walking trails, a pier, restaurants, and the popular Rocky Point Ice Cream.

🚗 Approx. 25 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps
🌳 Lake & Trails

Lafarge Lake

A peaceful Coquitlam destination with walking trails, gardens, water views, and seasonal events.

🚗 Approx. 30 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps
🌺 Botanical Garden

VanDusen Botanical Garden

A beautiful Vancouver garden featuring colourful flowers, walking paths, ponds, and seasonal displays.

🚗 Approx. 15 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps

🚗 Great Day Trips & Extended Adventures

🏔 World Famous Resort

Whistler Mountain

A world-renowned mountain destination known for skiing, hiking, beautiful scenery, shopping, restaurants, and outdoor adventures.

🚗 Approx. 2 hours from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps
🌸 Garden Paradise

Butchart Gardens

A world-famous garden near Victoria featuring spectacular flowers, walking paths, ponds, and beautiful seasonal displays.

⛴ Ferry trip required
🚗 Approx. 4 hours total travel time

📍 Google Maps
⛴ Island Destination

Victoria, British Columbia

British Columbia's capital city featuring the Inner Harbour, historic architecture, gardens, museums, and waterfront scenery.

⛴ Ferry trip required
🚗 Approx. 4 hours total travel time

📍 Google Maps
🏞 Canadian Rockies

Lake Louise

One of Canada's most breathtaking destinations. Famous for turquoise waters, mountain scenery, hiking trails, and the Canadian Rockies.

✈ Recommended: Fly to Calgary
🚗 Approx. 9–10 hours driving from Vancouver

📍 Google Maps
♨ Relaxation

Harrison Hot Springs

A peaceful lakeside destination known for natural hot springs, mountain views, beaches, boating, and relaxing getaways.

🚗 Approx. 1 hour 45 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown

📍 Google Maps

Vancouver & British Columbia Attractions

Explore some of British Columbia's most beautiful destinations before or after the Batang Nichols Reunion 2027. Click on any photo to view it full size.

Welcome to Beautiful British Columbia

Whether you're visiting from the Philippines or another province, Vancouver offers world-class parks, mountains, gardens, waterfronts, and unforgettable day trips.

🌆 Vancouver Highlights

🌊 Waterfront Landmark

Canada Place

Vancouver's iconic waterfront landmark featuring the famous white sails, cruise terminal, and spectacular harbour views.

🚗 Approximately 20 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps
🌲 Nature

Stanley Park

One of the world's great urban parks featuring the Seawall, beaches, forests, gardens, and Indigenous totem poles.

🚗 Approximately 25 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps
🏛 Historic District

Gastown

Vancouver's oldest neighbourhood, known for the famous Steam Clock, restaurants, boutiques, and cobblestone streets.

🚗 Approximately 20 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps
🎨 Public Market

Granville Island

Home to Vancouver's famous Public Market, artisan shops, restaurants, galleries, theatres, and waterfront views.

🚗 Approximately 20 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps
🌉 Adventure

Capilano Suspension Bridge

Walk across the famous suspension bridge, explore the Treetops Adventure and Cliffwalk through the rainforest.

🚗 Approximately 30 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps

🏔 Mountains & Nature

🏔 Mountain Adventure

Grouse Mountain

Ride the famous Skyride gondola, visit the wildlife refuge, watch lumberjack shows, hike scenic trails, or enjoy breathtaking views over Vancouver.

🚗 Approximately 35 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps
⛷ Scenic Mountain

Cypress Mountain

Known for spectacular viewpoints, winter skiing, summer hiking, and panoramic views of Vancouver and Howe Sound.

🚗 Approximately 45 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps
🌊 Waterfront Park

Rocky Point Park

A beautiful Port Moody waterfront park with walking trails, a long pier, playgrounds, restaurants and the famous Rocky Point Ice Cream.

🚗 Approximately 25 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps
🌳 Lake & Walking Trails

Lafarge Lake

A peaceful park in Coquitlam featuring a scenic lake, paved walking paths, gardens, fountains and beautiful seasonal displays.

🚗 Approximately 30 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps
🌺 Botanical Garden

VanDusen Botanical Garden

One of Vancouver's most beautiful botanical gardens, featuring thousands of plant species, peaceful ponds, sculptures and themed gardens.

🚗 Approximately 15 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps

🚗 Great Day Trips & Extended Adventures

🏔 World-Class Resort

Whistler Mountain

Home of the 2010 Winter Olympics, Whistler offers spectacular mountain scenery, gondola rides, hiking, shopping, restaurants, and one of Canada's premier ski resorts.

🚗 Approximately 2 hours from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps
🌸 World Famous Gardens

Butchart Gardens

One of Canada's most celebrated gardens featuring spectacular floral displays, fountains, walking paths, and seasonal events throughout the year.

⛴ Ferry required • Approximately 4 hours total travel time.

📍 Google Maps
⛴ BC's Capital City

Victoria

Visit the beautiful Inner Harbour, Parliament Buildings, Royal BC Museum, Fisherman's Wharf, and charming downtown Victoria.

⛴ Ferry required • Approximately 4 hours total travel time.

📍 Google Maps
🏞 Canadian Rockies

Lake Louise

World-famous turquoise lake surrounded by towering Rocky Mountains. One of Canada's most photographed natural attractions.

🚗 Approximately 9½ hours from Vancouver (ideal for an extended vacation).

📍 Google Maps
♨ Relax & Unwind

Harrison Hot Springs

A relaxing lakeside resort community famous for natural hot springs, beaches, boating, mountain scenery, and waterfront dining.

🚗 Approximately 1 hour 45 minutes from Hilton Vancouver Metrotown.

📍 Google Maps
